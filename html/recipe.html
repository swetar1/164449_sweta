Pasta

Easy to cook

Ingredients :
  • pasta
  • onion
  • pasta masala
  • green chillies
  • red powder
  • tomatoes
  • oregano
  • salt
  • cheese
  • butter
Steps to cook :
  1. boil the pasta
  2. chop the onions,chillies and tomatoes
  3. take a pan add 1tsp oil and add chillies to it.
  4. add onions and fry for two mins.
  5. add tomatoes and cook it for two mins
  6. add chiili powder,pasta masala, salt
  7. add pasta to it and mix it all and coo for three mins
  8. now add cheese
